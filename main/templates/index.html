Diversity & Inclusion

duPont Manual High School is proud to support our Black students, families, teachers, administrators, and staff. We believe that Black (BIPOC) lives do matter, and as such, we pledge to challenge inequities and improve outcomes for Black students in our school, city, state, and country; we prioritize safety and well-being of BIPOC.

Programs For Every Student

Dupont Manual High School offers a plethora of magnet programs that cater to diverse student interests and talents. Regardless if you like math or painting, Manual has a program for you!

{% comment %} TOP SECTION {% endcomment %}

MATH, SCIENCE, TECHNOLOGY (MST)

Unlock the world of innovation with advanced coursework in mathematics, science, and technology. Perfect for students passionate about problem-solving, scientific discovery, and cutting-edge technology.

HIGH SCHOOL UNIVERSITY (HSU)

Pursue a rigorous academic experience that mirrors a college environment. With opportunities for dual credit and advanced placement, this program is ideal for students focused on academic excellence.

{% comment %} BOTTOM SECTION {% endcomment %}

JOURNALISM & COMMUNICATION (J&C)

Channel your voice through writing, media production, and public speaking. This program equips students with the skills needed to excel in print, broadcast, and digital journalism.

YOUTH PERFORMING ARTS SCHOOL (YPAS)

Step into the spotlight with our dynamic Performing Arts program, offering specialized training in theater, music, and dance. Students hone their talents and perform in professional-level productions.

VISUAL ART (VA)

Explore your creativity and artistic expression through immersive, hands-on courses in painting, sculpture, photography, and more. A program designed for budding artists to refine their craft.

About Our School

Learn More

We are proud to be the best high school in Kentucky, consistently ranked #1 for academic excellence and innovation. Our students excel in rigorous coursework, cutting-edge technology, and extracurricular activities, preparing them for top universities and careers. With diverse magnet programs and dedicated faculty, we offer an enriching environment where every student can explore their passions and achieve their full potential.

Founded in 1889, Dupont Manual High School has a long-standing tradition of excellence in education. Originally established as a manual training school, we have evolved to become a hub of academic, artistic, and technological achievement. Over the decades, we’ve nurtured generations of leaders, thinkers, and innovators, adapting to the changing world while honoring our historic roots.
